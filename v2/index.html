Clients

(just a few of them)

  • Brightbox

    Brightbox is Europe's premier Ruby on Rails hosting provider. 3hv has an ongoing relationship, building and maintaining a number of their internal systems, including payment and billing.

  • Best Car Hire

    Best Car Hire is the best way to find a hire car. Mixing a slick, easy to use, interface and background processing of the price and location data from the car hire suppliers, the site was developed, in conjunction with Epiphany Solutions, in about a week.

  • Interim Partners

    Interim Partners, based in Harrogate and London, offer recruitment for Interim Managers - specialist, temporary, managers brought in to deal with change within an organisation. The Interim Partners web-site allows candidates to register their interest in interim and permanent positions, in different sectors across the globe, and notifies them as and when suitable vacancies become available.

  • Bowers Metrology

    Bowers Metrology supply UK industry with an unrivalled choice of high quality precision measuring equipment, bespoke system design and laboratory-based UKAS calibration services. In conjunction with Telescope Studios, 3hv supplied Bowers Metrology with a product catalogue web-site, taking data directly from Bowers' back-office systems. The site features a full content-management system allowing Bowers to update pages as and when required.

  • The Music Producer's Guild

    The Music Producer's Guild represents the interests of UK record producers, music recording engineers, mixers and anyone else directly involved in the production of recorded music. The site manages membership subscriptions and gives members access to exclusive content and offers.
